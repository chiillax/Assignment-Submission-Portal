Welcome to the Assignment Submission Portal!

If you already have an account, go ahead and log in. If you are new here, get started by creating a student account or a teacher account.

What's this about?

We started Assignment Portal for the Student-Teacher Community to provide the ease of online submission of assignments so that every time a new assignment pop-up or new semester starts , it's there for you already.

The Portal is designed to support TAs, students and Professors with submissions of Assignments on Time and keeps you posted. It is primarily designed by the students of IIITA , for IIITA.

The Portal contains the functionality of login and signup for students and teacher separately.The teacher can add assignments for the course they teach. They can also add up Instructions and required files for the assignment.

The students can add up the courses they are enrolled in and hence access the assignment of the course. They can submit the solutions to the assignment which are accessible for evaluation by the instructor of the course.
